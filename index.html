FAQ

What ages do you teach?
We offer a structured developmental syllabus designed for learners aged 7 to 25. Our curriculum is split into three main tracks: P.L.A.Y (ages 7-12) introducing visual blocks; G.R.O.W (ages 13-17) teaching text-based programming & electronics blueprints; and B.U.I.L.D (ages 19-25) preparing learners with modern machine learning and physical-computing applications.
Do I need prior experience?
Absolutely not! We welcome beginners across all age brackets. Our MIRAI Starter program is designed precisely to ease students in with intuitive block programming. For older absolute beginners, we provide bridging courses that help build essential logic principles before moving into text coding modules.
How do online classes work?
All classes are held virtually in live, interactive small groups led by our certified local learning advisors. We use interactive platforms, advanced virtual simulation software, and specialized online classroom tools. Students write code, debug circuitry virtual designs, and collaborate live, getting personalized focus just like an in-person workshop.
How do I choose the right programme?
You can use our integrated **Programme Advisor Quiz** right here on our website to get a quick tailored direction! Additionally, you can book an appointment to chat with our learning advisor. We will assess your child's age, logical background, and interests during a brief call to customize a future-ready curriculum layout for them.
